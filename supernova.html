SUPERNOVA

Fashion show

Get ready to strut your stuff on the catwalk! NHVPS is thrilled to announce its upcoming fashion show SUPERNOVA, where students will have the chance to unleash their creativity and style.

In this year’s ENIGMA, our annual fest, this fashion spectacle promises to dazzle and inspire.

Now, What sets this event apart?

Well, it's all about you – our talented students!

You'll have the freedom to choose from a range of captivating themes, allowing you to express your unique fashion sensibilities. Whether you're a trendsetter, a visionary designer, or a budding model, there's a place for you on our runway.

And here's the twist: you can go solo or team up with up to five fellow fashion enthusiasts to create something truly extraordinary. So, dust off those sketchbooks, raid your closets for inspiration, and get ready to make a statement. NHVPS’ Fashion Show, SUPERNOVA awaits, where creativity knows no bounds!

Register today!

Rules

  • NO PHONES OR ELECTRONIC DEVICES ALLOWED.
  • NO INAPPROPRIATE CLOTHES
  • No sleeveless
  • No shorts
  • No skirts above knee level
  • No see through clothes
  • No deep neckline clothes
  • No backless
  • No shirtless
  • No crop-tops
  • ANYONE WHO DOESN'T FOLLOW THE RULES WILL BE DISQUALIFIED.
  • Students are allowed to carry makeup for touch up. (Category B and C ONLY)
  • Category C ( 9 – 11) participants have to send their background music via g-mail or pen drive one day before the event.
  • Participants are supposed to change right after the event to school uniform

Event outline

  • Students can participate individually or as a group (2 to 5 members).
  • There will be two rounds, Round 1 will be held on the first day and round 2 (FINALS) will be held on the second day.
  • Participants will be given different themes to choose from. One team can only choose one theme per round. THE THEME CANNOT BE REPEATED FOR ROUND 2.
  • Students will be judged based on the creativity, relevance to the theme, the walk and confidence.
  • Category A and Category B will be expected to dress up from their house.
  • Category C have to get their outfits along with accessories to the school, they will be given an hour's time to dress up in the school. They will be expected to send their audios a day before the event and carry the same in a pen drive on the day of the event.
  • Students can carry props related to their theme.
  • Students are allowed to carry makeup for touch up.
  • There will be a GOLDEN BUZZER given to two team or participants in each category, who has the best outfit, walk and based on the way the present themselves. These participants will directly be promoted to the next round (FINALS).
  • Participants are supposed to carry school uniform with them. (students will have to change back to uniform after the event).
