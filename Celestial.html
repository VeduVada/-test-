Rules

  • The given topic must be followed.
  • Work should be done within the given time.
  • If the artwork is not related to the topic, the contestant will be eliminated.
  • No copying/referencing/inspiration will be allowed.
  • Participants will have to get their own materials as they deem fit after viewing the themes.
  • Only paper will be provided by the organizers.
  • Any break of the general code of conduct or ethical rules of the school and the competition will lead to immediate disqualification.

EventOutline

  • Students can participate individually only.
  • The event will have 2 rounds for all categories.
  • The judges can choose to group 2 participants as a team at their discretion.
  • For Category A:
    • Participants will be given topics on the spot and must draw from their imagination.
  • For Category B:
    • Round 1:
      • Each participant will be given an art sheet.
      • A vague description about a topic will be given to the contestants, and it is up to them to interpret this creatively in the form of an artwork.
      • The finalists of this round will be qualified for the second round.
    • Round 2:
      • Teams will be made by the event heads according to the finalists of the first round. Each team consists of two members.
      • Each team will have to choose a chit. The chit contains names of random objects that they will have to include in their artworks, using their creativity.
  • For Category C:
    • Round 1:
      • The teams will be made by the event heads. Each team will consist of two members and will be given an art sheet.
      • Only one member will get to paint at a time. The other member will be seated elsewhere. Team members keep changing every 15 minutes. Each contestant will get 30 minutes to complete their part.
    • Round 2:
      • Each member from the team will be blindfolded and will have to choose any one color from their box of paints.
      • Using only these 3 colors, the team members will be given a topic to follow, on which they have to create an artwork.
      • All teams will be given 10 minutes for discussion.
      • The members will switch every 20 minutes.
