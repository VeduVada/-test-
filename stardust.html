Rules

  • Eligibility: The talent show is open to all classes from I to XI.
  • Performance Style: Performances can be of any style/art form/talent but must be appropriate for a school setting.
  • Judging for Multi-Talent Performances: If a participant includes multiple aspects of their talent (e.g., playing guitar and singing), they will be judged accordingly.
  • Time Limit: Each performance must be limited to a maximum of 2.5 minutes.
  • Musical Selection: The chosen music should be appropriate for a general audience.
  • Respect & Sportsmanship: Participants and audience members are expected to maintain a respectful and supportive environment.
  • Background Music/Tracks: Participants using background music/tracks must:
    • Bring it on a USB drive (with a note) and submit it to the judges.
    • Alternatively, submit it online three days before the event.
  • Track Review: Judges can reject a track and request a redo/resubmission if it is deemed inappropriate or against the rules.
  • Dress Code: Participants must wear:
    • School uniform or traditional/unrevealing clothes.
    • Breach of dress code will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Day-of Event Issues: If participants turn up with inappropriate clothes/music, judges can choose not to allow them to participate.
  • Disregard of Rules: Any disregard of school or talent show rules will result in immediate disqualification.

Event outline

  • Students can participate individually.
  • There will be two rounds:
    • Round 1 will be held on the first day.
    • Round 2 (FINALS) will be held on the second day.
  • Participants can choose from different art forms or talents. In case of music/dance, the same song cannot be repeated for the final.
  • Students will be judged based on creativity, skill, experience, and quality of the performance.
  • For talents, participants are allowed to bring props.
  • The rounds will be judged by different experts in dance, singing, and instruments respectively.
  • Students are allowed to carry makeup for touch-ups (for dance only).
  • Participants are supposed to carry their school uniform with them and will have to change back into uniform after the event.
  • The event heads will open a link for the submission of tracks/dances 3 days before the event. Participants must submit their respective tracks/dances for verification. Physical copies can also be submitted within the given period.
